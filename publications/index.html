2025

  1. ManuscriptIn preparation

    Deep-Unrolled Max-Min Precoding for Cell-Free Massive MIMO

    Md Wazih Yeaser Tanzim

    Abstract: An unsupervised deep-unrolling network that maps estimated CSI to max-min-fair precoders for cell-free massive MIMO, trained label-free on a worst-user spectral-efficiency objective. The iterative max-min / LP-MMSE solver is reformulated as a differentiable unrolled architecture: regularized-inverse direction, projected-gradient spectral-efficiency ascent, and max-min power allocation.

  2. ManuscriptIn preparation

    Deep Learning for Hybrid Beamforming in Multi-User Cell-Free MIMO

    Md Wazih Yeaser Tanzim

    Abstract: A deep-learning precoder that predicts hybrid (analog phase-shifter plus digital) beamformers directly from estimated CSI under the non-convex unit-modulus constraint, targeting worst-user (max-min) spectral efficiency in multi-user cell-free MIMO.
