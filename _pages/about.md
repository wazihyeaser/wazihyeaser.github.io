I am an **AI Engineer at [Shottify](#)** and a **Research Assistant at the Committee for Advanced Studies and Research (CASR), [Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)](https://www.buet.ac.bd/)**, where I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering in 2024.

My research lives at the intersection of **wireless communications and machine learning**. I develop deep-learning and model-based methods — including **deep-unrolled** and **physics-informed** architectures — for the physical layer: **cell-free massive MIMO**, **hybrid and digital beamforming**, and **6G-and-beyond** systems. I am broadly interested in reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS), near-field and terahertz (sub-THz) communications, integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), and semantic communications.

I am currently preparing manuscripts on max-min-fair precoding for cell-free massive MIMO and am **seeking PhD opportunities** in wireless communications and machine learning for the physical layer.

Feel free to reach out — my email, Google Scholar, GitHub, and LinkedIn are linked below.
