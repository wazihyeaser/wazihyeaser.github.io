A deep-learning framework for **automated Bangla speaker recognition** and an **end-to-end speech-to-text** system targeting regional dialects — addressing accent variation underrepresented in standard Bangla ASR, with a focus on robustness to low-resource dialectal data and real-world acoustic conditions.

**Status.** In progress.
