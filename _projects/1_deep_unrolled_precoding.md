**Goal.** Map estimated CSI to **max-min-fair precoders** for cell-free massive MIMO with an unsupervised network trained **label-free** on a worst-user spectral-efficiency objective.

**Approach.** The iterative max-min / LP-MMSE solver is reformulated as a **differentiable deep-unrolled architecture**:

1. regularized-inverse direction,
2. projected-gradient spectral-efficiency ascent, and
3. max-min power allocation.

**Status.** Manuscript in preparation (targeting *IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications*).
