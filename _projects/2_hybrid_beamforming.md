A deep-learning precoder that predicts **hybrid (analog phase-shifter + digital) beamformers** directly from estimated CSI under the non-convex **unit-modulus constraint**, targeting worst-user (max-min) spectral efficiency in multi-user cell-free MIMO.

**Status.** In progress.
